Human Development Index and Gender Inequality Index

Name: Eline Rietdijk

Defenitions:

Human Development Index (HDI): A composite index measuring average achievement in three basic dimensions of human development :a long and healthy life, knowledge and a decent standard of living.

Gender Inequality Index: A composite measure reflecting inequality in achievement between women and men in three dimensions: reproductive health, empowerment and the labour market.

Data source Human Development Reports: HDI index, GII index
